Haramaya University (HRU)

Mission:

Haramaya University is dedicated to:
  • Training competent graduates across diverse fields of study.
  • Undertaking knowledge-generating, problem-solving, and cutting-edge research.
  • Providing demand-driven and transformative community engagement activities.

Vision:

By 2030, Haramaya University aspires to be among the top ten universities in Eastern Africa, recognized for its international reputation and excellence.

Goal:

HU aims to evolve into an autonomous and independent graduate and research university, achieving international standards in:
  • Academic Excellence
  • Research Innovation
  • Community Engagement

Motto:

Building the Basis for Development!
